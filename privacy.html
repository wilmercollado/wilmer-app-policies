Privacy Policy – Wilmer App

Last updated: August 13, 2025

Wilmer App (“we”, “our”, “us”) provides tools that read publicly available Instagram content from Business/Creator accounts and, with the user’s authorization, publish content to the user’s connected accounts.

Information We Collect

  • Account information you provide: name, email, and tokens granted through Meta to access your own Instagram Business/Creator account and connected Facebook Page.
  • Public Instagram data: public media metadata (e.g., media ID, caption, permalink, timestamp, media type) for specified public accounts.
  • Operational logs: timestamps of API calls, errors, and basic diagnostics to maintain service reliability.

How We Use Information

  • To detect newly published public media for automation workflows you configure.
  • To publish media to accounts you have authorized.
  • To comply with platform policies and applicable laws.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with service providers strictly to operate the service (e.g., hosting/storage) under confidentiality obligations, or as required by law.

Data Retention

Access tokens and logs are retained only as long as necessary to provide the service or as required by law. You may request deletion at any time (see “Data Deletion”).

Your Choices & Rights

  • Revoke permissions at any time in Facebook/Instagram settings.
  • Request a copy or deletion of your data by following the instructions on our Data Deletion page.
