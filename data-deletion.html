Data Deletion – Wilmer App

If you have used our app and want us to delete your data, please choose one of the methods below.

Quick Self-Service

  1. Go to Facebook/Instagram settings and remove our app’s access.
  2. This revokes tokens and stops any future access immediately.

Request Deletion from Our Systems

Send an email to carlos.calderon1022@gmail.com with the subject “Data Deletion Request – Wilmer App”. Include:

  • Your Instagram username and Facebook Page name (if applicable)
  • The email used with the app

We will confirm and delete associated tokens, logs, and stored files within 30 business days, and notify you when complete.

What We Delete

  • Access tokens, refresh tokens, and API credentials associated with your account
  • Operational logs linked to your account
  • Any stored media files uploaded by the automation (if applicable)
